# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 10, 2026

## 1. Introduction

SKYCOIN4444 ("we," "us," "our," or "Company") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- Email address and password for account creation
- Profile information (name, profile picture)
- Wallet addresses and transaction history
- Trading preferences and settings
- Support inquiries and feedback

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- Device type and operating system
- App usage analytics
- Crash reports and error logs
- IP address and location data (with permission)
- Device identifiers

### 2.3 Biometric Data
- Fingerprint or Face ID (stored locally on device, never transmitted)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Provide and improve our services
- Process transactions and maintain accounts
- Send security alerts and support messages
- Analyze app performance and user behavior
- Comply with legal obligations
- Prevent fraud and abuse

## 4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:
- End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
- Secure credential storage using device keychain
- SSL/TLS for all network communications
- Regular security audits
- Compliance with OWASP guidelines

## 5. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services:
- **Expo**: App development and push notifications
- **Google Play Services**: Analytics and authentication
- **Apple iCloud Keychain**: Secure credential storage

These services have their own privacy policies.

## 6. Data Retention

- Account data: Retained while account is active
- Transaction history: Retained for 7 years (legal requirement)
- Analytics data: Retained for 90 days
- Deleted data: Permanently removed within 30 days

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Request data deletion
- Opt-out of analytics
- Export your data

## 8. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover we have collected information from a child, we will delete it immediately.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of material changes via email or app notification.
